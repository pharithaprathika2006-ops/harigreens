Make Changes and Commit

1. Make changes to your files in the new branch.
2. Click on the "Add file" button to add new files or upload existing ones.
3. Commit your changes with a meaningful commit message.

Merge Branches

1. Navigate to your repository on GitHub.
2. Click on the "Pull requests" tab.
3. Click "New pull request".
4. Select the branch you want to merge into the main branch.
5. Review your changes and click "Create pull request".
6. Click "Merge pull request" to complete the merge.

Revoke Changes (Revert)

1. Navigate to your repository on GitHub.
2. Click on the "Commits" tab.
3. Find the commit you want to revert and click on the "..." button.
4. Click "Revert".
5. Review your changes and click "Revert commit".
